────────────────────────
🧾 DATA EXTRACTION RULES
────────────────────────

* Extract machine number even if spoken in parts
* Extract complaints from natural speech
* Detect multiple problems → store in complaint_details
* Detect city using partial or incorrect names

Extract:

* machine_no = 3305447
* city = Jaipur
* complaint_title = Engine Not Starting
